Murray

Being the "Friendliest" small town is something that comes naturally to this small university community. Consisting of a large retirement and family population mixed with university students, Murray provides a unique variety of customs and cultures nestled next to Kentucky Lake, offering sweet, southern charm. Murray knows no strangers. The town welcomes visitors into this small community with open arms and creates the feeling of being home.
